02 / ABOUT

From the signal on a wire

I’m a Computer Engineering student at Oral Roberts University, graduating in December 2026, with a focus on embedded systems, firmware, and hardware–software co-design.

What pulls me in is the full system: how power reaches a board, how sensors become bytes, how firmware turns those bytes into useful decisions, and how the whole design holds up under real constraints.

My work spans automotive electronics, CAN telemetry, PCB design, C/C++ software, controls, and GPU-based engineering research. I’m building toward a career creating dependable embedded products.
